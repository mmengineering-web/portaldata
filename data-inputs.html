Select YourSite Workbook

Choose the site you want to update. BM, TMS, and WKM are site names; each card opens the assigned OneDrive Excel workbook directly in the desktop Excel app when the device supports Microsoft Office protocol links.

Access requirement

BM, TMS, and WKM refer to individual operating sites. These workbooks use your shared OneDrive permissions, so users must be signed in with an authorized Microsoft account and have Excel installed on their computer.

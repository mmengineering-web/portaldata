Formula HPM Bijah Nikel — New Formula (Kepmen 144/2026)
HPM = [(%Ni × CFBijah Nikel × HMANikel)
     + (%Fe × CFBesi × HMABijih Besi × 100)
     + (%Co × CFKobalt × HMAKobalt)
     + (%Cr × CFKrom × HMABijih Krom × 100)] × (1−MC)

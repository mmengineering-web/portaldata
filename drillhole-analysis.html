DrillholeAnalysis

Analyze collar coordinates and assay intervals, review grade statistics, and visualize drillhole distribution for a Surpac-style drillhole assessment.

Analysis ScopeCSV
Required fieldsHole, X, Y, From, To, Ni
OutputsStats + Spatial
Review modeInteractive

Input Drillhole Data

Paste CSV data with headers: HoleID, X, Y, From, To, Ni, Fe. You can also load the sample dataset.

Intervals are length-weighted using To minus From. Spatial view plots collar distribution and colors points by average Ni grade.
